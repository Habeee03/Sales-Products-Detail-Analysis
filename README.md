# Sales Products Detail Analysis

The visualization presents an overview of sales performance across different regions, product categories, and time periods. It highlights key metrics such as total revenue, quantity sold, top-performing products, and sales trends, providing insights into customer preferences and overall business performance.

### The insights gotten from the analysis are:
- ♦️The dashboard shows that total revenue of $2,390.64 was generated from 62 units sold, indicating strong value per sale. Wakefield is the top-performing region, while Worcester contributes the least, highlighting uneven regional performance.

- ♦️Womenswear is the leading product category by a wide margin, followed by Menswear. At the product level, Formal Shirts stand out as both the highest in revenue and quantity sold, making them the key product.

- ♦️Sales performance by day reveals that revenue peaks on Wednesday and remains strong towards the weekend, while Monday records the lowest sales. However, the week-on-week analysis shows a significant decline in revenue after an initial increase, signaling a downward trend that requires attention.
  
In summary, the business is performing well in specific areas—particularly in womenswear, formal shirts, and the Wakefield region but faces challenges with declining weekly revenue and underperforming regions. Focusing on high-performing categories, optimizing weaker regions, and addressing the downward weekly trend will be essential for sustained growth.
